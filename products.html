Products and Services

We should provide more flexibility, based on insight, to better meet your needs.


Jute the 'Golden Fibre' of Bangladesh, has various inherent characteristics like, high tensile strength, low extensibility, long durability, fire and heat resistance, silkyness, luster and long staple length. Jute is available in abundant quantities. Jute is not only a major textile fibre, but is also a raw material for nontraditional and value-added products helping in environment protection and maintaining ecological balance. Besides, it has an enviable market - both local and international.





Product Specifications

The company manufactures yarn specific to requirement of the buyers
in terms of Quality Ratio(QR) and minimum QR Count Variation range and Strength Variation range.

  • Count Range: 4.8 lbs to 96 lbs Single & multiple ply.
  • Ply: 2 to 6 ply
  • Quality: SACKING, HESSIAN, CB, CRM, CRT, CRX, CRP.
  • Make-up: Bundle, Truss, Pallet Ball etc (Paper, Wooden. Cones and Core less spool.
  • Winding: Precision & Macroll.
  • Joint: Weaver’s knot, shaver’s knot, Spliced, latex
  • Packing: Stretch Wrapped Wooden Pallet.
  • Capacity: 100 M/Tons (Approx) of Jute Yarn & Twine per day (Three shifts).




Specialty:

High number of doubling in drawing
(4 stage drawing), hard district raw jute, standard packing, maintaining the rules of ISPM 15,eccelent
and accurate weights of mora that provides yarn regularity.
