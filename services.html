Exports

Bangladesh is the largest exporter of jute yarn/twine in the world and ABA Jute Mills & Twine Industries Ltd. contributing a healthy part by exporting 100% of its produced jute yarn/twine. Eventually, ABAJMTIL helping the world in becoming bio-degradable & eco-friendly by exporting our jute yarn.
ABAJMTIL has the strongest network of jute-yarn worldwide and kept its feet firmly in the International Market for several years. For several years, ABAJMTIL, dealt with many countries e.g. EUROPE, SOUTH-ARICA, EGYPT, ETHIOPIA, MOLDOVA, CHINA, RUSSIA, INDIA, ARABIAN GULF and the Far East etc. While working with our buyer, we never compromise with our quality and strictly follow our ethical trading policy.



Quality Objective

This Quality objective is issued for the concern of all Officers and Staffs of this mill to achieve target production with highest quality :


  • CV of Count 2%, CV of Strength 10% & Individual Min QR 100%
  • Difference between Maximum QR and Min QR should not be more than 20% to 30% (From 4.8 lbs. To 10 lbs. 30%. And above 10 lbs. 20%)
  • Customer complains will be reduce to 0% for (nil position) the every year.
  • New recruited workers should be trained “How to fix correct knot” by QA Department and HR Department before engaged.
  • All the mill manager should work together with Jute, Mechanical and Q.A Department to achieve target production with quality. ie. there should be no compromise with quality.


Service Process

Producer Identification

We identify new producers based on their product range, financial status, infrastructure, man power and efficiency of management. We continuously update our information on new manufacturers and new products. Information pertaining to new varieties of value additions, machinery, R&D department, quality control department and new technology area and availability of raw materials are also updated.

Selection of Producers

We assess the factories in terms of product range, quality of the product made, production capacity, facilities, financial capability, technology orientation, man power, working conditions, managerial efficiency and their overall quality policy. Once our initial assessment is over we exchange information with our principals and make a combined decision in approving an identified producer.

Sampling

Samples are developed to suit the specification, color and other terms if applicable of our buyer’s requirements. Our sample is available free of cost but the Buyer is suppose to pay the shipment charge. Therefore for providing sample we request our valued Buyers to ask any International Courier / Express service to pick up the sample from our office after confirmation of production of the sample.

Order execution and Quality Control: (Quality Management)

Orders are closely monitored from sampling to shipment. Status on sampling, production and shipment are transmitted to buyers regularly. We maintain a pro-active approach towards identification of problem areas and corrective measures are taken immediately to ensure timely deliveries. Our quality monitoring system pertaining to production has four stages as explained below:
Pre Production Check
Risks are reduced by checking raw materials / material components, accessories / user manuals etc. at this stage itself.
Initial Production Check
First finished products are checked against buyer’s specification and prototype sample. Deviations are identified and brought out for correction.
During Production Check
Inspection during production is carried out to check and verify that the initial discrepancies have been rectified and to ensure the average quality standard of production runs.
Pre Shipment Inspection (PSI) / Final Random Inspection (FRI)
Pre shipment inspection / Final random inspection is carried out when the total consignment is packed and ready for shipment. PSI / FRI is performed according to the International Inspection Standards and by any Internationally reputed survey organizations.
The detailed physical inspection of the samples selected at random is based on specifications of the buyers and it covers the criteria such as quality ration, breaking strength, TPI (Twist Per Inch), avg. count, C.V., moisture content, color, batch, slubs / hairiness, roots / specks, knots, spools sizes, Dia, oil content, weight, measurement, workmanship, packing etc.

Status Reporting

Status report based on the multi-state inspections is transmitted to the buyers by Fax or Email. Production progress is analyzed in relation to the delivery terms and suitable course of action is taken to ensure goods are delivered in time. The consignment is allowed for shipment if only it is up to the buyers required standard.

Container Stuffing and Loading Supervision

An identification of the goods during the loading is made to ensure that only the inspected goods are being stuffed without getting mixed up with the un-inspected goods. We also offer loading and tally supervision through any Internationally reputed third party organization. Certificate of which is also offered to our valued Buyers.

Documentation and Shipping Co-ordination

Shipping documents are checked as per the buyer’s instruction and the copy documents are sent to the buyers well in advance of the shipment to avoid any discrepancy.

Laboratory Testing

Laboratories are an extremely valuable component of our services and also facilitate the manufacturer – exporters to transact their business with least quality risk. Laboratory services include:
Raw Jute: Quality control team tries to test the raw jute to be confirm that actual quality is being used to produce the approved quality of the Jute product.
Color: Color is being tested to suit with approved samples.
Product: Breaking strength, quality ratio, C.V., Knots etc are being inspected to match with the approved sample.

Assists on Buyers visit

We work with Group Factories in Bangladesh. We accompany our buyers on their buying visits, factory visits and inspection visits. We help them with and arrange their itinerary and accommodation on their visits. We also orient the buyers on the does and don’ts of the business practice in the country, its culture, customs and tradition to make their visit fruitful and enjoyable.
All this at a cost which is actually a saving for our buyers! Yes, we reduce the cost of buying, cost of managing quality and cost of assuring deliveries. And, our customers get to know new developments, new products and new supply base from this part of the world.


Sample Policy

Asia Jute Sample Policies are supplying promotional Jute Bags & Jute Handicrafts, we receive much more sample requests than other businesses. We understand that a sample helps the decision making process, therefore we are happy to make and send samples. While we exempt sample fees in most cases, we cannot let slip the courier fees. To streamline and standardize the process, we outline our product sample policy below.
Pre-existing samples
A pre-existing sample can be a piece of sample material, a fabric color swatch, or a product picked up from our exhibition room or website gallery. Pre-existing samples do not incur origination, and all samples are charged for at their unit price. They will be sent out using our courier service DHL/FEDEX/UPS/EMS at a delivery charge of $50.00.
Pre-production samples
A pre-production sample can be a product prototype make to your design, or a matching sample emblazoned/branded with your artwork before mass production. When origination is incurred, we have to charge you $150(USD). We will reimburse you for the origination when you finalize an order over a certain amount.
Production samples
A production sample is a quality assurance sample randomly picked during or after production. You can come in person or we are happy to conduct a random sampling for you on site or at forwarders’ warehouse. Again production samples are free of charge.
Samples are free, but you are responsible for courier fees
If you require any of these samples being sent to you, please allow us to use your courier account. Or you can pay $50 (USD) for our courier service for a package weight under 0.5kg. We will choose from DHL, TNT, or FEDEX, EMS, UPS whichever fits the best in your area. For a sample package weight might be over 0.5kg, please contact us before making payment.
