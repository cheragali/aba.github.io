Company Overview

ABA JUTE MILL & TWINE INDUSTRIES LTD. was founded in 10th January 2010 by MD. ABU BASAR SIDDIQUE, Managing Director when he identified an opportunity to provide a practical solution to companies and consumers who wanted an ecological alternative to the plastic products.


ABA Jute is the leading Wholesale Manufacturer and Exporter of Jute Bag, Jute Shopping Bag, Jute Gunny Bag and Jute Sacking Bag. ABA Jute is committed to the development of jute products and eco friendly promotional services that further the creation of an ecologically sustainable future.





Mission, Vision & Core Values

ABA Jute is a full service vendor with strong vertically integrated production facilities as well as creative & analytical capabilities which clearly sets us apart from most other South Asian vendors.


Quality is the first priority with no compromise. We believe in continuing efforts to develop the quality by researching and analyzing.


Our Commitment: We are committed to ensure that we do not employ child labor in our factories, no workers are forced to work and employees are paid living wages. No discrimination is practiced, working environment is safe and hygienic and regular employment is provided. To that end we closely follow the ETI rules and regulations.
